Let's protect ourselves from the COVID-19

1. Good hygiene

2. Wash your hands frequently

3. Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your sleeve when you cough or sneeze

4. Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces

5. Avoid close contact with people

6. Avoid crowded places, especially indoors

7. Stay at home if you are sick, to help to stop the spread of whatever infection you may have
